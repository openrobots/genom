 \texttt{demoPosterLib.[ch]}  &  Posters libraries.\\
 \texttt{demoError.h}  &  Error codes (reports).\\
 \hline
 \multicolumn{2}{l}{} \\

 \hline
 \multicolumn{2}{|l|}{Test program:} \\
